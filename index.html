Welcome to Nawshin's Homepage

I am a first-year Ph.D. student and Doctoral Research Assistant in the Department of Computer Science at Texas State University, San Marcos, Texas, working in the Per4ML Lab (from Fall 2025) under the guidance of Dr. Tanzima Islam (Assistant Professor, CS, TXST). I am also a former Lecturer (currently on study leave) at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Dhaka, Bangladesh in the Department of CSE. I have completed B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from KUET. My primary research interest lies in High Performance Computing, Artifical Intelligence, Machine Learning Applications, Natural Languape Processing and Health Informatics. But I am always open to exploring new avenues and broadening my horizons by venturing into new fields of research.
